About The Project

              The HOSTEL DATABASE MANAGEMENT project is designed and developed to avoid manual maintenance of records in hostels and to make them automated. It will provide relief to the hostel administrators. This system will help in reporting, registration and searching of information of students and rooms. This system will provide faster access to the data and allow addition, upgradation, modification and deletion of data in systematic and reliable manner.
